About Me

Hello. I am Addy; a coder, a designer, & an artist.

I have been interested in creating with computers ever since I could remember. In those early days of my life, I would play around with HTML to make my Neopets' webpages & attempt to draw with my mouse on Paint. I always was curious & wanted to learn more. As I got older, I discovered Adobe suite products, like Photoshop & Illustrator. I also came to teach myself about video editing starting with the basics like Windows Movie Maker then Vegas Pro, Premiere Pro, & After Effects.

In 2014, I enrolled in OCAD University in their Digital Futures program. While there, I expanded my knowledge on many different aspects of design, art, & computing. I had also learned more about game design & development using Unity, 3D design using Blender, & coding using an Arduino.

I am currently enrolled in University of Toronto's Coding Bootcamp program, where I am delving deeper into coding & web development. My goal is to be able to master my skills, learn more, & meet more people who are also interested in the same things as me, all while maintaining a fun but professional atmosphere.

In this field I hope to make a positive impact on this world by making it a little more beautiful. As everything is becoming more available on online platforms, it is becoming increasingly more important to have the website we go to look clean, aesthetic, & easy to navigate.
